Background of the Organization

The VANI EDUCATIONAL ACADEMY is a voluntary organization initiated in the year 1997 under the society’s registration Act. The organization was established with a main motto that to serve to the Disabled people. The organization doesn’t confine to particular sections it spreads it activities to various type of categories l ike children, women groups and other needy groups mainly as a response to the situations like “The people especially, socially, economically backward and other vulnerable groups like tribes, particularly persons living with Disability and HIV/AIDS and Who are not in a position to enjoy right to equality, opportunity and right to live in urban & rural community”. In this context, some likeminded well educated persons (Doctors, Engineers, Advocate and other professionals) are come together under the leadership of Shri K.V.SRINIVASA RAO to change situation. As a result VANI EDUCATIONAL ACADEMY formatted with all the legal status. The founder and chief functionary Sri.K.V.SRINIVASA RAO having engineering back ground with post graduation in social science. He is very interested in Community building and has an experience in social service from Childhood onwards, the remaining in the group is also well educated, service oriented and committed to cause. And group itself came from rural background. Moreover the rules and regulations, functions and functionaries and their roles and responsibilities regarding the organization are well defined and clearly stated

Legal Position

1. Registered under the Societies Registration Act 21 of 1860 in the year 1997.
2. Registered under FCRA
3. Registered under Section 12A of income tax
4. Registered under sec.80 G of income tax act 1961
5. Registered under PWD Act as per section 52 of Persons With Disabilities (equal opportunities, protection of rights and full participation) act 1995
