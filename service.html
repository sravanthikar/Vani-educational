Vani Educational Academy

Vani Educational Academy is a non governmental organization striving for the social & economic empowerment of down trodden and neglected poor people of the community. Since 1997 its priority target group is persons with disability, HIV/AIDS and woman. This organization has received many awards from Government and others also. These organization members were nominated in Government committees like Ground Water Conservation Mission, Education Committee, Hospital development committee, District traning cell, District vigilance and monitoring committee etc.

Our Services

Special school for the deaf

Established in the year 1997 with one child and extended the services to 72 children. We are providing free Education, accommodation and clothing etc. with the financial assistance of the local philanthropist, NRI's, industrialists and individuals.

Special school for the child labour

Jaggaiahpet is 3rd grade municipality with having more slum areas, the livelihood of the community depending on cement factories, agricultural and building construction works. Running special school for child labour also..

Speech Therapy unit

Along with special school speech therapy unit also running for the deaf children to improve their voice/vocabulary.

Counselling and career guidance cell

Maintaining Counselling center for Childerns, Youth, Women.

Livelihoods training to poor and needy community

Income generation programmes like Readymade Garments manufacturing, cutting & tailoring, fashion designing, computers, soft toys making, fabric painting, entrepreneur programmes etc for youth and women.

Formation and nurturing of SHGs (Women, youth, Disability etc.)

Formation and nurturing of self help groups of woman, youth, disable, formers, and persons living with HIV/AIDS to promote sustainable livelihoods.working with line departments..
